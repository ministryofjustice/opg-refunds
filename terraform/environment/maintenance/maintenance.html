The Claim a power of attorney refund scheme has closed

You can no longer claim a refund on power of attorney fees. The refund scheme ran from 1 February 2018 to 1 February 2021.

If you’re waiting to hear the outcome of your claim

It takes up to 12 weeks for a claim to be processed. We’ll contact you to let you know the outcome, unless you asked us not to.
